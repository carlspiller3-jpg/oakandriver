## 🎨 Typography Concepts

The current typography was holding back the brand's potential. For a holding company specializing in acquisitions, the font should communicate **stability**, **heritage**, and **precision**.

### The Proposal: Modern Heritage
I've moved away from the thin, uneven serif in the original screenshot toward a more structured and elegant pairing:

| Element | Recommended Font | Rationale |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primary Title** | **Bodoni Moda** or **Playfair Display** | High-contrast serifs that scream "Executive" and "Premium". They feel established yet sharp. |
| **Subtitle/Holdings** | **Inter** (Wide Spacing) | A modern, clean sans-serif for "HOLDINGS" balances the heritage of the serif with a forward-thinking, professional edge. |
