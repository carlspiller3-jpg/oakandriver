Strategic Focus

Sectors of Interest

We are looking for established, profitable companies where our background in operations and growth can help the business reach its full potential.

Engineering

High Performance Manufacturing

We look for precision engineering and manufacturing firms with a reputation for quality. We want businesses that are technically excellent but need better commercial or operational structure to scale.

Infrastructure

Digital & Physical Systems

We are interested in the essential systems that keep things moving, from logistics to data. We like businesses with recurring customers and clear opportunities for system improvements.

Technology

Enterprise Software & AI

We look for B2B software and technology enabled services that solve real problems for businesses. We want platforms that are solid but could grow faster with better sales and leadership.

Consumer

Premium & Direct Brands

We look for premium brands with loyal customers. We help these companies grow by fixing internal operations and expanding their reach through better marketing and sales systems.
